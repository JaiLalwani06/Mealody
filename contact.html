Quick answers

When is Mealody launching? +

We're currently in early development. Join our waitlist on the homepage and you'll be among the first to know when we're ready!

Is Mealody really free? +

Yes! The free tier includes 3 meal plans per week, a basic grocery list, and access to our standard recipe library — forever free, no credit card needed.

Can I use Mealody for my whole family? +

Absolutely. Mealody Plus includes Family Mode, which supports up to 6 individual taste profiles and generates a combined meal plan that works for everyone.

Does Mealody support dietary restrictions? +

Yes — during setup you can specify allergies, intolerances, and dietary preferences (vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, etc.). Mealody will never suggest meals that don't fit your profile.
